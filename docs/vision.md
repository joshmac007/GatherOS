# GatherOS — vision

## North star

GatherOS is where your taste lives. Everything you save from anywhere
becomes one private, searchable library — and the app helps you turn that
library back into new work. Not a bookmark graveyard. A creative engine
that knows your eye.

## The four pillars

### 1. Capture from everywhere

One library, every source. X and Instagram today; Pinterest, Cosmos,
Are.na, Dribbble, Figma, and the open web next. Source-agnostic by
design — each save just carries where it came from, so a new platform is
a new adapter, not a new app. Local-first: your saves live on your
machine, dedup and deletions stick, and nothing breaks when a CDN link
expires.

### 2. A model of your taste

The library isn't a pile — it's a signal. GatherOS learns what you save
(color, composition, subject, embeddings) and can put words to your
aesthetic, surface what you keep coming back to, find the gap between what
you save and what you make, and resurface the right thing at the right
moment. Your taste, made legible — and shareable.

### 3. AI-powered moodboarding

Boards go from manual to generative. Start from a prompt or a seed save
and GatherOS pulls, arranges, and balances a board from your library —
then lets you remix, refill, and refine. The fastest path from "a feeling"
to "a board that captures it."

### 4. Generative design from your saves

The payoff: inspiration → artifact. Generate variations, palettes,
layouts, and components *in your taste*, grounded in what you've actually
collected. The library becomes the style guide the model designs against —
so the output looks like you, not like everyone's.

### 5. Public collections

Make a collection or board public as a shareable page. Follow other
people's collections, discover through taste rather than an algorithmic
feed, and let great public collections flow back into your own capture.
The social layer that makes the whole loop compound.

## Whatever else (on the roadmap)

- **Infinity view** — a spatial, infinite canvas of everything you've
  saved; zoom from the whole library down to a single pixel.
- **Universal search** — natural-language and visual search across the
  entire library, regardless of source.
- **Mobile + share sheet** — save from any app on your phone, pick up on
  desktop.
- **Taste profile** — your aesthetic as a single shareable artifact; the
  viral front door to everything above.

## What stays true

- **Local-first and private.** Your collection is yours, on your machine.
- **Taste-first, not feed-first.** We optimize for your eye, not engagement.
- **Fast and quiet.** Capture is one gesture; the app gets out of the way.

## Rough horizons

- **Now** — universal capture (X + Instagram shipped; Pinterest / Cosmos /
  Are.na next), durable local media, the combined "Saved" view.
- **Next** — the taste model and universal search; generative moodboarding
  on boards.
- **Later** — generative design from your saves; public collections and
  taste-based discovery.
