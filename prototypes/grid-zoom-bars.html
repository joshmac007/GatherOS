5 zoom bars for the grid

1 · Refined slider
The current pattern, tightened: small/large grid glyphs anchor the track, the app's pill thumb stays, and a value bubble appears on drag so you know the exact column count. Familiar, zero learning curve.

2 · Stepped notches
Discrete, click-anywhere notches — one per density step — read like an equalizer. No dragging precision needed, and the shape itself signals "small → large." Great for trackpad taps.

3 · Density presets
Opinionated, named sizes instead of a continuum — one tap and you're done. Fastest for people who think "I want it cozy," not "I want 5 columns." A segmented pill, like a view switcher.

4 · Zoom stepper
A − / + stepper with pips for the current level, like a map or PDF zoom. Precise, keyboard-friendly, and tiny — leaves the most room in a crowded toolbar. Click-and-hold territory for power users.

5 · Grid-size popover
Collapses to a single button; clicking opens a hover-to-pick mini grid (think Notion/Word's table picker). Visual, space-saving, and a little delightful — you literally draw the density you want.
